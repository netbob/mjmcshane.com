I majored in English at California State University Northridge and then worked for Walt Disney Productions. After several years of grad nights and “are you open until you close?” questions, I went to work for a computer company and worked as a warehouse manager, a senior manager of information technology and various program support and analyst positions throughout my career. Reading "The Iron Arm of Michael Glenn" kindled my desire to write when I was young.

I have been married for going on 41 years to an amazing woman who I am convinced is psychic. We have a daughter, Leslie who is getting her PhD from the University of California, Santa Cruz and has an amazing daughter and husband (hi Lee and Colin!) 

I primarily write historical, military, dystopian and technical fiction.  I am working on several novels based on real and imagined characters as well as trying to learn to play the ukulele. Four strings vs six right? D'oh!
